Custom Cabinets Are a Worthwhile Investment

Unlike prefab cabinets that are mass-produced and designed to fit a wide range of spaces (with little attention to detail), our custom cabinets are built specifically for your home. That means every piece is cut, assembled, and installed to match the exact measurements of your space.

Prefab cabinets from big-box stores often come with a lower price tag, but they're typically made from lower-quality materials, which may begin to warp, chip, or deteriorate within just a few years. Hardware is another often overlooked part of the cabinet systems -- screws and hinges also need to be high quality if your cabinet system is to last for decades.

With custom cabinets, you also have full control over the design. We've built traditional, colonia, shaker, and even contemporary cabinet systems. Beyond looks, custom cabinets improve your quality of life and efficiency. In the kitchen, for instance, custom cabinetry is designed to meet your cooking and storage needs. Every drawer and shelf is exactly where you need it. Say goodbye to the awkward gaps and odd placements that often come with prefab options!

We focus on the small details that make a big difference, such as dovetail joints, soft-close hinges, and carefully matched finishes. Whether you're looking to update your home office, kitchen, or any other room, call us.
