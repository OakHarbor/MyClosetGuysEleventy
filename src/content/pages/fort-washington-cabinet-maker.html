FAQs Regarding Custom Cabinets in Fort Washington

What Makes Your Custom Cabinets Different From Big Box Store Options?

Our cabinets are tailored to fit your space, your style, and your day-to-day needs. Big box store cabinets are made in bulk, often using cheap materials and one-size-fits-all dimensions. We build each cabinet from scratch using high-quality materials and a personalized design process. You get a system that looks better, functions better, and lasts much longer.

Can You Build Cabinets For Odd-Shaped Rooms?

Yes, we specialize in custom designs that work in rooms with sloped ceilings, unusual corners, or tight layouts. We take careful measurements and create 3D renderings that let you preview how everything will fit before we build. Whether you're working with a compact bedroom or a narrow mudroom, we'll make it feel intentional and efficient.

How Long Does The Process Take From Design To Installation?

Most cabinet projects are completed in three to six weeks. That includes the initial consultation, design, materials, fabrication, and installation. If your project includes several rooms or complex cabinetry, it might take a little longer. We'll always be upfront with your timeline so you know what to expect.

What Types Of Materials Do You Use?

We use high-grade commercial MDF for most of our cabinetry, especially when clients prefer a painted finish. This material gives us crisp edges, a smooth surface, and greater stability than standard plywood. If you prefer hardwood or another specialty material, we're happy to accommodate that as well.

Is Commercial MDF Better Than Plywood For Cabinets?

For most residential cabinet projects, yes. MDF is more stable than plywood and resists warping better, especially in high-moisture environments like bathrooms. It also takes paint more evenly, giving you a flawless finish. While plywood is sometimes used for structural framing, MDF is our top choice for cabinets that need to perform well and look great over time.

Do You Handle All The Work In-House?

Yes, we manage everything from the first consultation to the final installation. We don't hand off work to third-party installers or outsourced builders. That means you're working with the same expert team throughout the project, with consistent communication and no surprises.

Can You Match Existing Cabinet Finishes?

We can usually match or closely coordinate with your existing finishes. Whether you're adding a new cabinet to an older kitchen or trying to refresh part of a bathroom, we can create a look that blends seamlessly. If you'd rather start from scratch with a new finish, we can show you plenty of options too.

Do You Build Cabinets For Garages Or Utility Areas?

Yes, we build tough and functional cabinets for garages, laundry rooms, and utility spaces. These systems can include heavy-duty shelving, wall-mounted storage, pull-out bins, and more. We take the same care with utility areas as we do with kitchens and bathrooms, so your cabinets will hold up and stay organized.

Absolutely. We offer custom pull-out trays, deep drawers, vertical dividers, spice racks, soft-close hinges, and other upgrades that make your cabinets more user-friendly.
