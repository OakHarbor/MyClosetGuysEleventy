Custom Closet FAQs

What Is Included in a Custom Closet Consultation With My Closet Guys?

Our free design consultation includes a walkthrough of your space, measurements, and a conversation about how you use the closet. We'll discuss what kind of storage you need, such as double hanging, drawers, open shelving, or built-ins. We'll also talk about finishes, accessories, and layout options. You'll receive a custom 3D rendering so you can visualize everything before moving forward.

How Long Does It Take to Design and Install a Custom Closet?

Design typically takes just a few days after the consultation, depending on how quickly you choose your finishes and layout. Once approved, we begin building in our shop and then schedule your installation. Most installations are completed in one day. Larger or more complex systems may take longer. We give you a clear timeline before any work begins.

How Much Does a Custom Closet Cost?

Every closet project we take on is completely custom, so prices vary depending on size, materials, layout, and accessories. A small reach-in system could start around $1,200 to $1,800, while larger walk-ins or full dressing rooms may range from $3,500 to $7,000 or more. We provide a detailed, no-obligation quote after your consultation and can discuss financing options if needed.

What Materials Do You Use for Closet Systems?

We primarily use commercial-grade MDF, which is more stable and long-lasting than plywood or particle board. It provides a smooth finish, resists warping, and allows for consistent color and texture across all surfaces. For clients who request it, we also offer hardwood upgrades and a wide selection of textured laminates and custom veneers.

Are Your Closets Built On-Site or in a Factory?

All of our closet systems are built in our own shop right here in the Maryland area. This allows us to maintain quality control, accommodate last-minute adjustments, and get your system installed faster. Nothing is mass-produced or flat-packed like the big-box alternatives.

Do You Remove the Old Closet System First?

Yes, we handle full removal of your existing closet system before installing the new one. That includes any wire shelving, anchors, brackets, or pre-installed rods. We prep the wall and make sure it is clean and ready for your new custom system. You won't need to hire another contractor or worry about demolition.

What Types of Closets Do You Design?

We design all types of closets, from small hallway reach-ins to expansive walk-in closets, linen closets, kids' wardrobes, and even built-ins for guest rooms. We also specialize in converting unused rooms or awkward spaces into functional storage areas. Every design is based on your space, lifestyle, and preferences.

Can You Match My Existing Cabinetry or Trim?

In most cases, yes. We offer a wide range of finishes, drawer styles, and hardware options that can match or complement your home's existing décor. Whether your home features traditional wood tones or modern matte textures, we help you select a look that blends seamlessly with the rest of your space.

Do You Offer Closet Accessories Like Lighting or Jewelry Drawers?

Absolutely. We can integrate LED lighting, valet rods, tilt-out hampers, jewelry drawers, pull-out belt racks, and more. If you've seen a feature you love online or in another home, let us know and we'll explore how to incorporate it into your design. We also help prioritize accessories based on your daily routine and budget.
