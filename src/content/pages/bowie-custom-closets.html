Our Custom Closet Design and Build Process

Firstly, we will schedule a time when our expert designer can visit your home, measure the space, discuss your unique goals, and offer sample materials. This will be a collaborative process, and we love to incorporate our customers’ feedback.

Secondly, we’ll take those design ideas back to the office and create a 3D model for you to review. We will continue fine-tuning the design until you are excited and ready to start building.

After the design is ready to go, we’ll start assembling materials, cutting boards to perfect dimensions, and organizing all of the hardware and dividers we’ll need during the installation process. We want things to go as quickly as possible once we arrive at your home with the closet modules.

After the product is ready to go, we’ll decide on a time to visit your home and install your beautiful custom closet. We are very careful with your furniture, carpet, paintings, and walls, and we’ll use protective sheets when necessary. We realize this can feel intrusive to homeowners, and we promise to be in and out of your house as quickly as we can while maintaining our standard of quality.
