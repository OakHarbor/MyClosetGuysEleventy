You'll Love Working With My Closet Guys

Our experience and craftsmanship shine through with every project. After our initial phone call, we'll schedule a complimentary design consultation at the job site. We will discuss your needs, get a feel for your aesthetic preferences, show your material samples if you'd like, and take measurements. We will also want to know what you plan on storing -- we may need to take weight into consideration.

We will then create a 3D mockup of the project for you to approve. Only after we've hammered out the details will you receive a quote from us. Once you've signed the quote, we'll start building the storage components in our workshop.

The installation is about as simple as it gets. Everything is custom cut and measured in our workshop, so we can usually install your garage storage system and be out of your way within a few hours.

From your first interaction with us until we finish the project, we are committed to a friendly, knowledgeable, and collaborative experience for our clients.
