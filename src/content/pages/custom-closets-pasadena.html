What's It Like to Work with My Closet Guys in Pasadena?

We believe every closet should feel tailor-made. Because it is. From the first phone call to the final screw in the wall, our process is smooth, creative, and built around you.

We Start with a Friendly Consultation

Every project begins with a conversation. During our initial call, we'll talk about the closet (s) you'd like to upgrade. Maybe you're dreaming about a walk-in with elegant storage for bags and shoes. Maybe you need a sleek reach-in solution with drawers, rods, and adjustable shelving. Whatever your goal, we'll learn about your preferences, frustrations, and ideas so we can offer thoughtful solutions right away.

We Visit Your Home in Pasadena

Our designer will come to your home to take accurate measurements and evaluate the space. We'll look at ceiling height, wall structure, outlet locations, and any other unique features. We also bring material samples, so you can touch and see what your future closet might look like, from wood grain to finish to hardware details. This is when the collaboration really starts to take shape.

We Create a Free 3D Design

We don't expect you to imagine your closet from a sketch. We use advanced software to produce a full-color 3D rendering that brings your vision to life. Want to adjust the color? Try a different drawer configuration? Explore soft-close doors or hidden hampers? We'll revise the design until it's exactly what you want. The 3D rendering is yours to keep, and there's no cost or obligation to move forward.

We Build and Install With Professional Expertise

Once you approve your design, our team cuts and builds each piece in our workshop. Every part of your closet system is custom-measured for your home, which means installation is usually fast (often done in a few hours). We protect your floors and furniture while we work and treat your home with respect from start to finish.
