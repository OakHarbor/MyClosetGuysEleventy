Forget Big Box Store Cabinet Systems

You can do better than a prefab cabinet system. The first issue with prefab systems is durability. Most are made with extremely cheap materials like particleboard or plywood, and these can warp, crack, and sag after just a few years. The hardware holding the system together matters as well -- cheap screws and hinges lead to the untimely demise of many cabinets. You should also take the installation into account, and not just because of looks! It can be dangerous to have a cheap cabinet system on your wall. Installing cabinets is an art form unto itself, and it needs to be executed perfectly.
