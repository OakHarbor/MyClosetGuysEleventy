Custom Pantry Shelving, Cabinets & Design in Maryland

My Closet Guys, the leading home storage system company in Maryland, helps improve your quality of life with fully customizable pantry solutions. From pantry cabinets to shelving, built-ins and more, we can design the perfect pantry for your lifestyle. Get in touch for your free design consultation today - we would be happy to hear from you.

You'll Love Working With Our Pantry Storage Experts

We have over 40 years of experience in the industry, and we've done it all: walk-in pantries, reach-in solutions for economical kitchens, wine racks, bread box built-ins, and so much more.

In our initial design consultation, we'll want to know more about your specific needs. How often do you cook, how many cubic feet of storage do you need, do you want room for an island in your pantry area, and so forth. We will take measurements and provide materials examples for your review.

Then, we'll create a 3D version of your pantry in our design software. We will present you with the pantry design, and you will be able to send us feedback and workshop the ideas along with our team. After you have approved the design, we can proceed with a full, itemized quote for you to review.

After you've approved the quote, we'll custom build each piece of the pantry system in our shop. We cut the boards, implement hardware choices, and measure each piece to exact specifications. Because of this attention to detail, we can usually install the panty in your home in just a few hours.

If you have any questions about our process, capabilities, or anything else, please send us a note.

Help With Pantries of All Shapes and Sizes in Maryland

Small Pantry Design in Maryland

Limited space does not mean limited functionality. For small, reach-in pantries, My Closet Guys designs intelligent solutions that optimize space, such as vertical storage systems, pull-out shelves, or door-mounted racks. These systems ensure easy access and visibility to all stored items, reducing clutter and creating an organized pantry. You can choose between open or closed shelving, pull-out drawers, corner shelves, or built-in spice racks. The team can even incorporate wine racks, specialty bins for potatoes and onions, and even counter areas for small appliances.

Large Pantries

For spacious walk-in pantries, My Closet Guys can create a bespoke pantry system complete with dedicated zones for different food types or purposes. For instance, there could be a specific area for baking supplies, a shelf for canned goods, or a corner dedicated to snacks. We can even include glass-fronted cabinets for showcasing beautiful china or glassware. Pull-out baskets can be used for fruits and vegetables, allowing for ventilation and easy access.
