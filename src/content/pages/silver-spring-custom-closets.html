Kinds of Closets We Can Design and Build

Master Bedroom Walk-In Closets

Firstly, consider the overall size and layout of the closet. A spacious walk-in closet should offer enough room to move around comfortably and accommodate your wardrobe with room to grow. The layout should maximize storage and provide easy access to all areas. U-shaped and L-shaped layouts are popular choices that optimize space and allow for a variety of storage options.

Incorporate a mix of storage solutions such as hanging rods, shelves, drawers, and cubbies to accommodate different types of clothing and accessories. Adjustable shelving and rods provide flexibility to adapt the space as your wardrobe changes over time. Ensure that the closet has ample lighting to make finding items easier and to showcase your collection - we can even work with you on installing LED lights for energy efficiency and longevity.

Furthermore, a well-designed walk-in closet should also feature a dedicated area for shoes, with slanted or flat shelves to keep them organized and easily accessible. Adding a full-length mirror will not only create the illusion of a larger space but also provide a convenient spot to check your outfit before leaving the room. For added functionality, consider incorporating a seating area or a small island with storage for folding clothes, packing luggage, or laying out outfits.

Lastly, personalize your walk-in closet with decorative touches such as wallpaper, paint, or artwork to make the space feel inviting and reflect your style. The kinds of trim, molding, doors, and more are something we will discuss with you to make sure we reach your goals.

Reach-In and Linen Closets

Perhaps you need to reimagine a current hallway closet with additional shelving or storage space, or maybe you have a few square feet in your bathroom where we can install a custom linen closet. Our reach-in closet designs are intended to maximize every square inch, both horizontally and vertically, and you'll be amazed at how much clutter you can get rid of with a professionally done reach-in closet.

Custom Wardrobes and Built-Ins

If you don't want to allocate the amount of space necessary for a walk-in closet, but you want to elevate your bedroom or guestroom, we can help you by creating custom built-ins along your wall, a custom wardrobe that will keep your clothes out of the way and safe, and more. Unique Touches To Make Your Closet Beautiful

Custom Doors To Elevate Your Closet

Sliding doors, which glide horizontally on tracks, are ideal for smaller spaces as they don't require additional room to swing open. Sliding doors are available in various materials like wood, glass, and mirrored surfaces, allowing you to choose a style that complements your closet's design. We can also include various high end finishes like soft-close functionality.

Bifold doors, popular in our reach-in closet designs, consist of two panels that fold together when opened, requiring less clearance than traditional swing doors. They are available in various materials, including wood, glass, and metal, and can be customized with different finishes or decorative elements.

We also work with a variety of hinged, swinging doors depending on the style of your closet. Hinged doors can be made from various materials, including solid wood, glass, and MDF, and can be customized with panel designs, molding, and hardware to match your aesthetic preferences.

Don't forget French doors! They create a wider entrance and add an elegant touch to your custom closet. They can be styled with different types of glass, like frosted or patterned, for added privacy and design flair.

Pocket doors are another fun option. They slide into a recessed compartment within the wall, making them a great space-saving option for small rooms. They can be customized with a variety of materials, such as wood or glass, and can feature different designs, including raised panels or sleek, modern styles. These are an excellent choice for modernist aesthetics.

We also install barn doors in walk-in closets for more rustic homes. Barn doors are mounted on a track above the doorway and slide horizontally, creating an industrial aesthetic. They are available in various materials, including wood and metal, and can be customized with different finishes, textures, and hardware.

High End Shelving and Storage Solutions

Custom cabinetry crafted from premium materials like solid hardwoods or sleek, lacquered finishes not only adds durability but also exudes sophistication. Consider incorporating glass-front cabinets or LED-illuminated shelves to showcase your most prized possessions and create an upscale boutique feel. Soft-close drawers and pull-out trays with velvet lining protect delicate items while adding a touch of elegance. Modular systems with adjustable shelving and hanging rods allow for flexibility in accommodating wardrobe changes. Integrating high-end accessories like rotating shoe racks, pull-out pant racks, and jewelry organizers completes the lavish walk-in closet experience.

Consider a Luxurious Closet Island

High-end walk-in closet islands offer a luxurious centerpiece for your space, combining style and functionality. Premium materials like marble or quartz countertops elevate the island's aesthetic, while custom cabinetry provides ample storage. A seating area or cushioned bench enhances the island's usability, creating a comfortable spot to relax, lay out outfits, or try on shoes.
