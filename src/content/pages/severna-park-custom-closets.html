We Can Work With Your Favorite Wood

Many of our customers prefer to use plywood for their custom closet because of its versatility and cost-effectiveness. We can make plywood look beautiful, and load-bearing is not usually an issue because we can reinforce your shelving.

However, if you want a higher-end look and feel, we can also use various types of natural wood. Pine is a readily-available option, and it adds a nice smell to your closet. The same applies to cedar, another popular wood for closets and accents.

Do consider the fact that natural wood may split or bend if the humidity levels fluctuate in your closet. If you think this might be the case, speak with us about building your custom closet out of plywood with reinforcements or another popular material, melamine.

That said, we do not need to use one single material for the whole system. We can create a cedar space for your wool clothes, and construct the rest out of a different material, for instance. Just let us know what your preference is, and we are always happy to make suggestions.
