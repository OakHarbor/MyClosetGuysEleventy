Our Closet Design and Installation Process

What To Expect When You Call or Email Us

When you get in touch, you can expect a friendly chat with one of the owners about your closet or other project. We’ll ask questions about the size of the space, the goals you have for your new closet, and perhaps the materials you’d like to use.

We will then schedule a visit. We will measure the space and bring sample materials, and we’ll gather all of the information we need to start designing your custom closet.

Your Free Quote

We will then send you a free and personalized quote for the project that includes materials, costs, and labor. At this point, you are more than welcome to ask us questions about the cost, time frame, or anything else. We value customer service and communication.

We Design Before Building

You will be able to view a full 3D model of your closet before we start to build the modules and cut the wood. We want you to be happy with the design, material selection, and space allocation before we proceed - we can always make adjustments to the layout during this phase of the project.

Building and Installing Your Closet

Once you are excited about the design of your closet, we’ll start cutting the boards and building. We use high quality materials and professional tools, and we make sure everything is cut well and will fit perfectly. This keeps sliding shelves and other things functioning perfectly. Our installation process is quick and painless - we are often in and out of your home within a day.

We also welcome any feedback our customers have after the installation is done. We want you to be happy with your custom closet, and we’ll make sure things are done right every time.
