Styles We Work With

Here at My Closet Guys, we work with several styles of cabinets to offer you the best possible option for your home.

Traditional

Traditional style cabinets are usually decorated with intricate details like raised panel doors, ornate molding, and rich wooden finishes. These cabinets are often made out of cherry, oak, or mahogany, and they greatly add to the elegance of any room.

Art Deco

Characterized by bold geometric shapes, vibrant colors, and luxurious materials, art deco incorporates mirrored surfaces, lacquered finishes, and chrome or brass accents. This style is meant to create a glamorous and sophisticated look.

Complementary Design Styles

The goal of complementary styles is to work with the existing style of the room. Homeowners will often request minimalist cabinets with clean lines and neutral colors to complement modern styles.
