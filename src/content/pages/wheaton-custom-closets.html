Our Custom Closet Process

First, you can expect a friendly chat where we understand your designated closet space, your design preferences, your use case (children's closet? Master bedroom walk-in closet? Linen closet?), and gather any other useful bits of information.

After that, our design professional will visit your home and take measurements. We will understand the depth, width and height of your space, and we'll show you potential colors, materials, and finishes. We would also love to learn more about your vision for the closet. If you aren't sure, that's fine as well -- we can guide the design process.

Next, we will mock up a 3D model of your future closet for your review. This step is still complimentary, and you can send us feedback for us to work into the model. Only after you've approved the mockup and design will we send you a quote.

After you've reviewed and approved our quote, we'll take the fabrication process to our workshop. We custom cut every board, drawer, and shelf, and we use state-of-the-art tools to measure with great precision. This allows us to quickly install the closet system in your home and be out of your hair as quickly as possible.
