Options For Your Dedicated Laundry Space

Laundry Room Shelving Systems in Maryland

One of our most sought-after features is our built-in shelving, tailored to fit the dimensions of your laundry room. Whether it's a small space or a large room, we take advantage of every square inch to offer as much storage as possible. We offer various materials such as sturdy melamine, sleek metal, or timeless hardwood, each providing unique visual appeal and durability. We can create a custom floor-based shelving system or add beautiful and minimalistic floating shelves - we will take your needs and preferences into account.

A Space For Every Washer and Dryer

We understand that different homes have different laundry appliance configurations. Whether you have a stacked washer-dryer set for space efficiency or standalone units, we design the layout to accommodate your appliances perfectly. We also ensure that these spaces have proper ventilation and are easily accessible for maintenance or repairs.

Have Space For a Laundry Folding Area?

For larger laundry rooms, we can incorporate a dedicated laundry folding area. This can include a smooth, wide countertop over your standalone washer and dryer or a separate folding station. We offer a variety of countertop materials, from practical laminates to luxurious quartz or granite, depending on your budget and style.

Too often, laundry spills out of the laundry room onto your bed, the dining room table, or elsewhere to be folded when you have time. Let us declutter your home and improve your lifestyle with a pleasant and economical laundry area in your home.

Verticality Counteracts a Small Laundry Room

For smaller laundry rooms, vertical storage solutions can be a game-changer. By utilizing the room's height, we create additional storage areas without taking up valuable floor space. This can include tall shelving units, wall-mounted cabinets, or even overhead storage, all designed to hold laundry essentials like detergent, fabric softener, and cleaning supplies.

Need Your Laundry Storage System to Be Accessible?

In every laundry room we design, ease of use and accessibility are our top priorities. We ensure all shelves and cabinets are within easy reach, and the appliances are positioned for easy loading and unloading. Drawers and pull-out baskets can be included at lower levels to store laundry or cleaning items, and raised platforms can be designed for ergonomic positioning of your washer and dryer.

Practical Extras

To elevate your laundry room further, we offer additional features like built-in ironing boards, drying racks, or even a utility sink for hand-washing delicate items. We also consider effective lighting, ensuring there is ample illumination in all areas, from appliance spaces to storage shelves.
