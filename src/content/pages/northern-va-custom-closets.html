We Visit Your Home and Take Measurements

The next step is for our expert closet designers in Northern VA to visit your home, take measurements of the space in question, measure the ceiling height, and locate studs, electrical outlets and more. This helps us give you an accurate quote and build the closet system perfectly the first time.

Considerations When Building Your Custom Closet in Northern Virginia

Your Wardrobe: Current and Future

Don't risk outgrowing your custom closet in the near future. Take into consideration your shoes, suits, dresses, jewelry, watches, bedding, and anything else that you would like to have easy access to. Take a look around your bedroom - what clutter would be resolved by a closet system built specifically for you? We can install angled shoe shelves, locked watch drawers, a beautiful jewelry display, and so much more.

Functionality and Accessibility

Do you want to have a luxurious space to get dressed each morning? We can create comfortable bench seating (or leave room for chairs). Furthermore, we can maximize space by building storage above and below the seating areas.

Some of our clients enjoy having a closet island, sometimes complete with built-in seating and/or drawers. Mirrors, lighting, and accessibility for elderly or wheelchair bound folks are all things we can discuss in our consultation.

The Impact of the Closet on your Home's Resale Value

If the property is going to be your home for the long haul, resale value probably isn't your primary consideration. But if you might be moving in a few years, consider the impact of the custom closet on future buyers.

That said, walk-in closets, custom built-ins, or linen closets can only increase your home's resale value.
