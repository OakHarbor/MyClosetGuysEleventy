Custom Closet Design For the Power Suit Collector

If you would like to showcase your suits, access them more easily as you prepare for meetings or a day at the office, and keep them safe from moths and dust, we can design a custom closet system for you. Wood selection weighs into the equation, and we can also add space for ties, shoes, shirts, and more.

Cedar is a popular choice for suit storage, as its natural oils and scent act as a repellent for moths and other insects. Cedar also helps absorb moisture, which can prevent mold and mildew from developing on your suits. Another option is sandalwood, which has similar insect-repellent properties to cedar and also gives off a pleasant aroma. Additionally, mahogany is a durable and strong wood that can provide excellent protection for your suits. No matter which type of wood you choose, it's important to keep your suits in a cool, dry place to prevent damage and extend their lifespan.
